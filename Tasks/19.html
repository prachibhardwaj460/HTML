About College

Lovely Professional University is one of India's leading private universities, located in Phagwara, Punjab. Established in 2005, LPU is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and doctoral programs in engineering, management, science, arts, law, pharmacy, design, agriculture, and many other disciplines. The university is known for its modern campus, experienced faculty, advanced laboratories, digital learning facilities, and strong focus on research and innovation. LPU also provides excellent placement opportunities through collaborations with leading national and international companies. Along with academics, the university encourages students to participate in sports, cultural events, entrepreneurship, and community service for their overall personality development. LPU aims to provide quality education, practical learning, and global exposure, helping students build successful careers and become responsible professionals.
